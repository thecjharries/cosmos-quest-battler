Cosmos Quest Battle Solver

This tool was inspired by a REPL I found on the forums. I wanted a weekend project and this seemed fun. It's still very much a WIP, and I won't guarantee any of the results until I loop back around for testing.

There's not much in the way of elegance here. Beginning with teams of one unit, I check all possible permutations. To reduce computation, I save the outcome of all unsuccessful combats and use those as starting points for the next stage. If at any stage a permutation is successful, I remove it. Unsurprisingly, this leads to a ton of solutions for simples quests, so I track the most and least expensive solutions at each stage. In theory, there are 406 permutations, which is surprisingly small. In practice, a little planning reduces that tremendously.
